What's the point?

Those who have built a site do not experience it in the same way that many of their users do. At the same time, many people don't know how that experience differs and how to check it. Accessibility has sadly been an industry afterthought for a while - not focused on in courses or degrees, and no clear checks or practices to follow. That's where I come in.
